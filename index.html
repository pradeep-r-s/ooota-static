About Us

At Ooota, we believe good food should reach people without extra burden. Unlike traditional delivery platforms, Ooota follows a Zero Commission Model. Local restaurants thrive, customers save money, and food remains authentic.

Vision

To become India’s most trusted, zero-commission food delivery platform, empowering local eateries and creating value for every customer.

Mission

To revolutionize food delivery with fairness, transparency, and trust, ensuring both restaurants and customers benefit equally.
