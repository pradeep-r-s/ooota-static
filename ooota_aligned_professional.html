About Ooota

We believe good food should reach people without extra burden. Unlike traditional platforms, Ooota follows a zero commission model designed to help local restaurants grow while customers enjoy authentic food at fair prices.

  • For Customers: Same restaurant prices, delivered. No hidden fees.
  • For Restaurants: Grow volume without losing margins to high commissions.
  • Community: Local restaurants thrive, customers save, and food stays authentic.
